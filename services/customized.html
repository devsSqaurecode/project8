WHY SHOULD YOUR COMPANY COLLABORATE WITH AN INCUBATED COMPANY AND NOT THE INSTITUTE?

A case for B2B collaboration.
These incubated companies can be “hired” to develop and refine nascent technology, patents developed at the institute. Their physical presence helps you company to create an efficient and bi-directional communication channel for a long-term partnership.

Omnipresent - every educational and research institute has at-least one incubation centre that has a capacity to incubate at 15 to 20 companies at a given time. Many, especially IITs, have two to five incubation centres sponsored by various ministries, who find IITs a safe investment.

Omniscience - The promoters have total understanding since they are insiders.

Omnipotent - Incubated company has the right knowledge and commitment coupled with an urgency to deliver solutions for your problems.

We can help you identify and initiate the collaboration.
